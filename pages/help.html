Help & FAQ

Find answers to common questions

Getting Started

How do I create a new task?

Click on "Add Task" in the navigation menu. Fill in the task title, description, category, priority, and due date. Then click "Create Task" to save it.

How do I edit an existing task?

Go to "All Tasks" and click on the task you want to edit. You can modify any field and click "Save Changes" to update it.

Can I delete a task?

Yes, you can delete tasks from the task details page or all tasks page. Deleted tasks go to the trash and can be recovered within 30 days.

How do I mark a task as complete?

Click on a task and select "Mark as Complete" or check the checkbox on the task card in the all tasks view.

Organization & Filtering

What are task categories?

Categories help organize your tasks by type (Work, Personal, Shopping, Health, Education, Other). You can filter tasks by category to focus on what matters.

What do the priority levels mean?

High: Urgent tasks that need immediate attention. Medium: Important but not urgent. Low: Tasks that can be done anytime.

How do I search for tasks?

Use the search box on the "All Tasks" page to find tasks by title or description. Search works across all your tasks.

Can I filter tasks by due date?

Yes, visit the "Due Date" page to filter tasks by today, tomorrow, this week, this month, or overdue tasks.

Data & Settings

Where is my data stored?

All your tasks are stored locally in your browser using LocalStorage. This means your data stays on your device and is never sent to any server.

Will I lose my tasks if I clear browser data?

Yes, clearing your browser cache or LocalStorage will delete all tasks. Be careful when clearing browser data.

Can I export my tasks?

Yes, go to your Profile page and click "Export All Tasks" to download your tasks as a JSON file for backup.

How do I import previously exported tasks?

On the Profile page, click "Import Tasks" and select a previously exported JSON file to restore your tasks.

Can I change the theme?

Yes, go to your Profile settings and select Light, Dark, or Auto theme based on your preference.

Troubleshooting

My tasks disappeared. What happened?

This usually happens when browser data is cleared. Check your trash folder first. If not there, the data may have been cleared from LocalStorage.

Why aren't my changes saving?

Make sure your browser has LocalStorage enabled. Some browsers disable it in private/incognito mode.

The app isn't working properly. What should I do?

Try refreshing the page. If the problem persists, clear your browser cache and reload the application.

Can I use Task Manager on mobile?

Yes, Task Manager is fully responsive and works on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.

Tips & Tricks

How can I stay productive?

Start each day by reviewing your dashboard. Set realistic goals, prioritize important tasks, and celebrate completing them by marking them complete.

What's the best way to use categories?

Organize tasks by major life areas (Work, Personal, Health, etc). This makes it easier to focus on one area at a time.

Should I use tags?

Tags are optional but helpful for grouping related tasks. Use tags like "urgent," "bug," "feature" to make searching easier.
